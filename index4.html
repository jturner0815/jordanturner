About This Dashboard

This Retail & Warehouse Sales Performance Dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of sales trends, supplier contributions, and predictive insights. It visualizes key metrics such as total retail sales, warehouse sales, and retail transfers over time, enabling trend identification. Supplier performance is highlighted through bar charts and a detailed sales breakdown, while clustering and ABC categorization segment sales based on value distribution. Additionally, predictive analytics compare actual and forecasted retail sales. This dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making, helping businesses optimize inventory, track supplier impact, and forecast sales trends effectively. Built using Power BI with advanced data visualization techniques.
